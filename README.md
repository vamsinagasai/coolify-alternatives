# Best Coolify Alternatives in 2026

Coolify is a popular self-hosted PaaS that lets you deploy apps on your own VPS. But self-hosting means you are responsible for server setup, security patches, updates, and everything that breaks at 2am. For teams who want the simplicity of Coolify without the server management overhead, there is a better option in 2026.

## Why Developers Look for Coolify Alternatives

- **Server management burden.** Coolify runs on your own VPS. You provision it, maintain it, and fix it when it breaks.
- **No autoscaling.** Traffic spikes require manual intervention or over-provisioning.
- **Upgrade risk.** Coolify updates can break running deployments. You manage the upgrade cycle.
- **No built-in monitoring.** You wire up external tools for alerting and observability.
- **Account termination risk.** If your VPS provider suspends your account, everything goes down.
